Covid-19 Tracker by State

- Track New York travel restriction guidelines on each state.

- Data from The COVID Tracking Project.

- Note: Table may not match up with NY Travel Restriction list of states; the restriction list is updated weekly, while the table is updated daily.

The criteria for NY 14-day quarantine are:

> 10 positive cases per day per 100k residents based on the 7 day rolling count.

> 10% positivity rate.

How to use tracker:

- The table will display the number of positive cases per day per 100k residents and the positivity rate.

- If both numbers are below 10, then travelers from that state do not to quarantine.

- The states that do not need to quarantine are shown in green.

- The states that do need to quarantine are shown in red.
